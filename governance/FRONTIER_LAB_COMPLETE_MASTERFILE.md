| Phase | Duration | Gate | Deliverable |
|-------|----------|------|-------------|

| 3. Literature Search | Days 4-6 | G2 | ≥15 studies identified |

| 7. Manuscript Assembly | Days 21-23 | G5 | Draft manuscript |
