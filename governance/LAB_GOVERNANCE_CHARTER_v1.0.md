# Frontier Lab Governance Charter v1.0

**Effective Date**: 2026-03-11  
**Authority**: Lab Director  
**Scope**: All research activities, agent operations, and manuscript production

---

## Core Governance Principles

1. **Scientific Rigor**: All outputs must be evidence-grounded and methodologically sound
2. **Operational Excellence**: Systems must be reproducible, auditable, and efficient
3. **Strategic Alignment**: Work must advance institutional and research mission
4. **Continuous Learning**: Each session generates skills that persist across sessions
5. **Three-System Persistence**: All artifacts exist simultaneously in GitHub, Obsidian, Google Drive

---

## Stage Gate System (G0-G7)

| Gate | Criterion | Authority | Pass/Fail |

| G0 | Topic aligned to active pipeline | Automated | 24h |

## Quality Standards

All outputs must satisfy:
1. **Scientifically rigorous** (evidence-grounded, methodologically sound)
2. **Operationally useful** (decision-oriented, actionable)
3. **Strategically meaningful** (aligned with institutional mission)
